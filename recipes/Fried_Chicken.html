Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

Fried chicken is a staple food of any american family, and you can get it at a lot of fast food restaurants. The recipe is really simple to make, and I think you will have a really enjoyable time making this with the family. Also, there will probably be lots of leftovers with this dish, so you will have food for days.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • butter
  • pan

Steps

  1. Slaughter the chicken
  2. Smother butter all over the dead chicken
  3. Fry the buttered chicken in the pan
