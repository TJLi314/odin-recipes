Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a delightfully tasteful dish that is a staple of any Italian dinner and probably any American dinner. The recipe is really simple to make, and I think you will have a really enjoyable time making this with the family. Also, there will probably be lots of leftovers with this dish, so you will have food for days.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • marinara sauce
  • milk

Steps

  1. Make the flour into lasagna shaped pasta ready to bake
  2. Pour the marinara sauce all over the lasagna
  3. Dunk the finished product in milk
