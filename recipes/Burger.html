Burger Recipe

Description

Burgers are my favorite American food, and they're sooooo delicious, especially the double decker ones. The recipe is really simple to make, and I think you will have a really enjoyable time making this with the family. Also, there will probably be lots of leftovers with this dish, so you will have food for days.

Ingredients

  • 2 beef patties
  • buns
  • tomatoes
  • lettuce
  • pickles
  • onions
  • swiss cheese

Steps

  1. gill the two patties
  2. place cheese over the patties and put them in between the bun
  3. place lettuce in between the two patties
  4. place pickles on the bottom bun
  5. place tomatoes and onions under the top bun
